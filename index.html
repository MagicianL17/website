Welcome to The Gizmo Dojo!

The gizmo dojo provides space and community for members to develop projects and grow their passion for making. We are a member-operated 501(c)(3) non-profit community space. We welcome creators, hackers, and makers of any kind.

About the space

With have several stations throughout the space including a 3D printing area, an electronics station, a textiles area, and laser cutting. We also have a wood shop and a welding area.

You can see patial a list of the tools that are available here.

The heart of the gizmo dojo is a central common area where members gather to socialize, collaborate on products, and learn.

Become a member

If you are interested in becoming a member please come by our open house on saturdays from 2-4 pm or our member meetings on mondays from 7-9pm and a member of our team can give you a tour and walk you through the joining process. If those times don't work for you, you can email us at info@thegizmodojo.com to request a time that works for you and we'd be happy to accomidate you.

If you would like you can print and fill out a membership form beforehand here.
